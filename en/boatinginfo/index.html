Corporate boating

Corporate boating - a unique opportunity to unite the working team and get a lot of pleasant travelling experience. Nowadays we have our own possibilities for the organization of the corporate boating up to 60 people (double kayaks and canoes, manufacture of England, France, and Belarus). We can offer routes of different difficulty levels throughout Belarus, boating from several hours to several days (for large groups we recommend one or two days boating), qualified  instructors of the Greenteam, the necessary equipment (paddles, life jackets, hydrobags).

See photogallery

* On the right bank of the river Western Berezina works the eco-camping " Zastsenak Skrypleva ." The opportunity to organize a 3-hour corporate boating followed by rest on the territory of the abovementioned eco-camping.

Additional proposals:
- transfer to the place of the start and back
- catering
- tourist bath (sauna)
- Installation and dismantling of a campground
- hire of tents and of other camping equipment*
- hire of the sound amplifier
- hire of current generators
- Technical transport service
* Only in case of ordering service " installation of the campground "

Organization of the entertainment part of the program (optional) *
- installation of various level of complexity of a hinged obstacle course
- the mobile rock climbing wall, height – 7 meters, 2 paths
- shooting range (pneumatics and bow)
- water competitions on kayaks and canoe
- refereeing, marking and installation of platforms for minisoccer and volleyball
- organization of evening entertainment programs (rock and folk groups, dancing collectives, DJ)

Bands: Krama, NRM, Krambambulya, Apple tea, bard Z.Vaytsyushkevich, Pivo vdvoyom, etc. Fairy Dance dancing group, folk-groups Na Taku, Rada, Goryn.
