Eco-camping Zastyanak Skrypleu

Camping "Zaścianak Skrypleŭ" is located 90 km from Minsk, on the right bank of the river Western Berezina. Camping is part of the farmstead "Zaścianak Skrypleŭ."
Camping territory is 1 ha. The camping area is equipped with a shelter from the rain, the fireplace and woods, car parking, toilets, drinking water, wood-fired sauna.

The cost of an overnight stay in your  tent – 4 EUR/person

The cost of an overnight stay in your  tent – 6 EUR/person

A bath (sauna), 2.5 hours  - 50 EUR/ 110 BYR. (with a broom)
